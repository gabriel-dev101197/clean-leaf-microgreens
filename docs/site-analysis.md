# Clean Leaf Microgreens — Site Analysis & Design Brief

## Brand Overview
- **Name:** Clean Leaf Microgreens
- **Location:** Bristow, Virginia
- **Type:** Family-owned microgreen farm
- **Instagram:** @cleanleafmicrogreens

## Mission
Provide freshly cut, nutrient-dense microgreen varieties to the community without the use of pesticides.

## Target Audiences
1. **Home Consumers** — Health-conscious individuals looking to elevate everyday meals
2. **Restaurants & Chefs** — Farm-to-table experience, custom/bulk orders
3. **Farmer's Market Shoppers** — Local community members

## Unique Selling Propositions (USPs)
1. Grown to order — maximum freshness guaranteed
2. 100% pesticide-free / organic growing practices
3. Family-owned, local farm (Bristow, VA)
4. Serves both home cooks and professional chefs
5. Nutrient-dense superfoods (up to 40x more nutrients than mature plants)

## Design Direction for New Landing Page
- **Style:** Clean, organic, modern — earthy greens with fresh white space
- **Primary Color:** Deep green (#2D5016) with leaf-inspired accent (#7CB342)
- **Typography:** Modern sans-serif for headings, readable body font
- **Photography Style:** Bright, close-up macro shots of microgreens
- **CRO Focus:** Clear CTAs, social proof, urgency, benefit-driven copy
- **SEO Focus:** Schema markup, semantic HTML, optimized meta tags, fast load
